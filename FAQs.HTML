Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I reset my LMS password?
Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
Can I access LMS on mobile?
Yes! The LMS works on all mobile devices through browsers, and a mobile app is also available.
How do I submit assignments?
Visit your course, open the "Assignments" section, and follow the instructions to upload your file.
Where can I view my grades?
Grades are shown in the "Grades" tab of each course after your work has been reviewed by the instructor.
